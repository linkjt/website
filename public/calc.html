Calculator Projects

Projects for both the TI-84+CE and TI-Nspire CX II. CE projects utilize the CE-Toolchain, being writen in C and compiled into Z80-ASM. All project elements are entirely made by me. I am part of both the Cemetech and TI-planet communities.

TI-WARS

This was the first game I ever made, it is in TI-BASIC. It has over 500 downloads and is hosted on Cemetech. It is a railshooter that is star-wars themed.

CKEX

Second game I made, writen in ICE, compiled into EZ80ASM. It has over 1000 downloads and is my most popular game. It is a calculator fighting simulator.

TI-Coin

Calculator based crypto currency that utilizes SHA-0. It is created and mined locally, however there are plans to open it up. Writen in C, it is the most popular non-game project.
